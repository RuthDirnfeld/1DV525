Who is Hodor?

Hodor is overjoyed by basic things like presenting Bran with a new saddle or hearing the echo of his own voice. Despite his large size and strength, Hodor is a gentle soul who is terrified by violence - even violence doneby himself, as he often cowers in fear instead of fighting back against foes who are actually smaller than he is .

Personality

  • Peaceful
  • Good-hearted
  • Loyal

Abilities

Hodor is not only peaceful and good-hearted, but also very:

  1. Strong
  2. Powerful

He is able to carry Bran on his back over long distances with no visible signs of fatigue, and lift a full-grown man off the ground and break his neck with minimal effort.
